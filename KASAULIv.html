About Kasauli

Kasauli is situated at an altitude of 1927 meters and like a few other places in Himachal Pradesh, reflects the influence of colonial rule. The views from here are spectacular and this is also a well known health resort. An laid back vacation is best enjoyed amidst the scenic views this place has to offer. It is recommended to stay here for a day to explore the place properly.

Famous for: Hill Station, Viewpoints, British colonial period. Major Attractions: Kasauli Brewery, Sunset Point. Nearby Places: Solan, Chail, Shimla.


How to Reach

AIR:
Chandigarh Airport (IXC) is the nearest airport with better connectivity. It takes about 1 hr 51 min (58.1 km) from here to reach Kasauli via road.

RAIL:
The nearest station is in Sonwara (SWO), 6 kms away from Kasauli. Kalka railway station, 40 kms away is the next best option for tourists as it connects other important sites via Kalka-Shimla railway line.

ROAD:
